About E-Ink Design

The goal of this web page is to recreate the distinctive aesthetic of e-ink displays, reminiscent of classic e-readers like the Kindle. This design philosophy emphasizes readability, simplicity, and a paper-like quality that's easy on the eyes.

Design Principles

Typography

Serif fonts enhance readability and provide a classic book-like feel.

Clean, well-spaced text reduces eye strain during long reading sessions.

Color Palette

High contrast black text on off-white backgrounds.

Minimal use of color, focusing on grayscale tones.
